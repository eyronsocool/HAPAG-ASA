How HAPAG-ASA Works ‚Äî Guidelines

Welcome! These quick guidelines explain how to use the HAPAG-ASA portal to browse programs, apply for assistance, or donate. If you have any questions, contact us at hapag-asa@gmail.com.

  1. Sign up / Log in: Click Sign Up to create an account or use the profile icon to log in. Use a valid email so we can verify your account.
  2. Browse Programs: Visit Programs to see active feeding programs, schedules, and eligibility requirements.
  3. Apply for Assistance: On a program page, click Beneficiary and complete the application form. Provide necessary details and attach any required documents.
  4. Donate: Go to the Donate page to contribute funds or food packs. Follow the instructions for secure submission.
  5. Get Help: For system issues, account support, or urgent questions, email hapag-asa@gmail.com or use the profile contact options.

MISSION

To empower barangays in Olongapo City through digital technology by connecting them with individuals, organizations, and sponsors, thereby enabling sustainable and transparent feeding programs and nutrition initiatives that eradicate hunger and improve community well-being.

VISION

A hunger-free Olongapo City where every barangay is equipped, supported, and united through technology and bayanihan spirit to ensure all families have consistent access to sufficient, nutritious food for a healthy and thriving community.

HAPAG-ASA

Connecting communities to fight hunger in Olongapo City through transparent, community-driven feeding programs.
